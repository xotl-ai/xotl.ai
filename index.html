Xotl

A private research operation focused on capital allocation.

Independent financial research powered by artificial intelligence. Tracking macro trends, energy markets, geopolitics, and equities, synthesizing signal from noise in real time.

Approach

Xotl works from primary sources: official data releases, regulatory filings, energy and shipping flows, central bank communications, and market pricing, rather than secondhand commentary. The goal is a clear, evidence-led picture of how capital moves through the global economy.

Artificial intelligence handles the volume. It reads and organizes large amounts of source material, flags connections across markets that are easy to miss by hand, and keeps a running, time-stamped record of how each thesis develops. The judgment stays human. People set the questions and stand behind the conclusions.

Coverage

Macro
Growth, inflation, rates, liquidity, and currencies. These set the cost of capital and the backdrop for every other market.
Energy
Oil, gas, power, and the materials and infrastructure behind the energy transition, where physical supply meets financial markets.
Geopolitics
Trade, conflict, sanctions, and policy, plus the second-order effects they have on supply, prices, and the movement of capital.
Equities
Individual companies and sectors, studied from the bottom up and connected back to the macro and geopolitical picture.
